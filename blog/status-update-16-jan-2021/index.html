Status Update, 16 Jan

Jan 16 2021

I’ve been too busy to write for a while, and in fact I’m still busy. I spent the whole day designing the database architecture of a project, and suddenly I realized that it’s snowing outside. It wasn’t snowing like this for the last 2-3 winters in Istanbul, and honestly, I feel like a child who is waiting in front of the window, watching it like a miracle. I wish there could be a way to gather all of our wives, husbands, fathers, mothers, friends, relatives, anyone who is important and just play with the snow without hesitating tomorrow.
